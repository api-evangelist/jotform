If you’ve built a Wix website for a nonprofit, fundraiser, school, or important cause, you may be looking for a simple way to accept donations online. Wix offers two ways to do this: Wix Donations and Wix Forms. Wix Donations is a native app built for running fundraising campaigns in Wix.
